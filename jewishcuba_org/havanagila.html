Havana Nagila

A collection of materials on the award-winning documentary by Laura Paull and Evan Garelle.

The Making of Havana Nagila  Laura Paull discusses the complexities of making a movie in an embargoed country, and how she and her husband used the Internet as a major resource.

Movie Reviews  Reviews of Havana Nagila, and Abraham and Eugenia, two movies focused on the Jews of Cuba.

Review by Ruth Behar  Behar's perceptive analysis of Havana Nagila, and commentary on the potential problems of U.S. citizens visiting Jewish communities there.

Review by Michael Fox  Fox analyzes Havana Nagila from another perspective.

Hava Nagila, Lyrics and Melody  The classic Chasidic melody, with lyrics by M. Nathanson.
