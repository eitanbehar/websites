Sephardic Cemetery Monument to the 6 Million

The Jewish Community of Cuba
erects this monument at the behest of the Bet-Haim society
in memory of the millions of our brothers
sacrificed by the Nazi Fascist barbarism
in the years of its sinister domination of Europe.
Havana, March 1947.
Central Council of the Jewish Communities of Cuba.
