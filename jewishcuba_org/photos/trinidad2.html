Maylea and Clara Elena, children of Octavio and Marlene, in my casa particular in Trinidad.



A typical tourist breakfast in the casa for someone paying in dollars. Eggs and coffee are on the way.
A typical breakfast for Cubans is bread, butter, and cafe con leche.


Ice cream time on the street. Cubans eat lots of ice cream, and out of respect I frequently observed this custom.


A young man inside his house. His puppy ran under the bed before I could get a photo.


A multi-purpose, multi-passenger bicycle.


Baseball in the park. In Cuba any space will do for a game.
