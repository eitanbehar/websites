At the age of 15, young Cuban girls are treated royally, with parties, photos, and much recognition.
This is known as the "quinces."



Night-time Trinidad. Like in much of Cuba, the evening is a good time to sit outside and talk.



Trinidad's "Western" section, where horses are tied to the front of houses.



There is a lot to be said in support of this lifestyle. I know many people who would gladly trade residences.



Many people in Trinidad keep birds as pets. You hear a lot of birdsong when you walk around the town.
